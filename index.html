Description

Fauzhan Wahyudi is a motivated junior web developer with a solid foundation in JavaScript, C, and storage algorithms. He recently earned his bachelor's degree from Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology, where he completed his thesis on "Implementation of S3-FIFO Buffer Logic in FEMU."

FEMU, a flash-storage emulation program based on QEMU, features an innovative implementation that leverages the black-box functionality to utilize logical page numbers for filtering unique entries. Fauzhan is eager to apply his skills and contribute to dynamic development projects.

  • Smart Drying Rack

    The drying rack can be controlled via Telegram using several commands, such as "lift" to raise the drying rack, "dry" to extend it into sunlight, and "status" to retrieve data on light intensity and rainfall. This allows users to determine whether the drying conditions are suitable for drying clothes.

  • HATI - HATI

    An application that visualizes historical criminal events in a specific location on a map, featuring predictive parameters for potential criminal activity at a given time, derived from a Machine Learning model. With "Hati-Hati," we aim to empower the Indonesian community, particularly women and children, to identify safe times for visiting certain areas and avoid becoming victims of crime. This project is a collaborative effort by a team of students from Bangkit Academy 2022.
